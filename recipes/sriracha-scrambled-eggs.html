Sriracha-Scrambled-Eggs

Start your morning with a burst of flavor with our Sriracha Scrambled Eggs! This breakfast delight combines

the creamy richness of scrambled eggs with the bold kick of sriracha sauce, creating a perfect harmony of

heat and savory goodness. Quick and easy to whip up, these eggs are sure to elevate your breakfast game and

leave your taste buds craving more. Spice up your morning routine and enjoy a delicious twist on a classic favorite!

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • tomatoes (optional)
  • shredded cheese (optional)
