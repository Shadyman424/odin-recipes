Honey Garlic Chicken

Enjoy the perfect balance of sweet and savory with our Easy Honey Garlic recipe.

Quick to make, this delightful dish brings together honey and garlic in a flavorful glaze.

Elevate your meals effortlessly with this delicious creation.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Season chicken : pat the chicken breasts dry and season with salt and pepper.

    2. .

  2. Sear chicken : Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken breasts until golden brown on both sides, about 4-5 minutes per side.

    3. .

  3. Prepare Sauce : In a small bowl, mix honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, and grated ginger.

    4. .

  4. Coat Chicken : Pour the honey garlic sauce over the seared chicken.

    5. .

  5. Simmer : Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.

    6. .

  6. Glaze Chicken : Occasionally spoon the sauce over the chicken to glaze it.

    7. .

  7. Serve : Once the chicken is cooked, serve it over rice or your preferred side dish.

    8. .

  8. Garnish (Optional) : Sprinkle sesame seeds and chopped green onions on top for added flavor and presentation.

    9. .

  9. Enjoy : Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious and easy Honey Garlic Chicken!
